About Me

I am a 3D Artist passionate about designing and sculpting characters that feel authentic, expressive, and visually compelling. My work focuses on transforming ideas into fully realized 3D model.

I specialize in both character and environments, sculpting, and texturing, with proficiency in industry-standard tools such as Blender/Maya, ZBrush, 3D Substance Painter, Nuke and Photoshop.

Coming from a background in accounting and digital studies, I bring a unique perspective to my art: structured problem solving, meticulous attention to detail, and a drive for continual growth. These qualities help me approach complex design challenges with creativity, discipline, and efficiency.

Beyond technical skill, I thrive in collaborative environments where ideas can evolve and strengthen through teamwork. I am eager to contribute to projects that push artistic boundaries and bring imaginative worlds to life through character-driven design.

When I'm not working on 3D projects, I enjoy exploring visual storytelling through illustration, cultural influences, and design these are the sources of inspiration that continually enrich my creative approach.
